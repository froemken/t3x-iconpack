============
Contribution
============

Every good idea and extension depends on whether it is used, accepted by the
community and whether it is financially supported. Therefore...

Contribute | Say Thanks!
========================

- If you like this extension, **use it**.
- If you think you can do something better, be so kind and `contribute your part
  to it <https://github.com/quellenform/t3x-iconpack>`_
- If you love the extension or if you want to support further development,
  **donate** an amount of your choice:

.. rst-class:: horizbuttons-tip-m

- `Become a sponsor of this project and make further developments possible!
  <https://www.paypal.me/quellenform>`_
